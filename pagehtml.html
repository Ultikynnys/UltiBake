What is this?

Ultibake overhauls Blender baking completely while taking inspiration from Substance Painter and Marmoset Toolbag.

Features

  • Multi Object Baking - Bake entire collections at once.
  • Profiles - Save and load your baking setups to external files.
  • Multi Texture Baking - Bake multiple textures at once with a single click.
  • Channel Packing - Specify which channel to use when baking masks, just like in Substance Painter.
  • Decal Baking - Bake any decal to the surface of your desired object. Ideal with DecalMachine, Sanctus Library, and Stamp It!
  • High to Low Baking - Project the surface of a high-detail mesh to a low-poly representation.
  • 16-bit Baking - For special use cases like exporting height maps.
  • Special Bakes - Bake ColorIDs, Ambient Occlusion, Curvature, Thickness masks, and more!
  • Dynamic Suffix Generator - Automatically generates texture suffixes by analyzing the Baking Setup.
  • Post-Bake Image Cleanup - Apply effects like antialiasing, denoising, sharpening, and more after baking.
  • Search - Minimize visual clutter when using multiple bake setups.
  • UDIMs - Automatic UDIM baking and tile resolution controls.
  • Finalize - Create "export-ready" formats of baked objects with one click.

Quick Start

How does it work?

The add-on will bake any material utilizing the Principled BSDF node.

The add-on will bake Principled BSDF inputs as Bake Types along with Special Bake Types.
